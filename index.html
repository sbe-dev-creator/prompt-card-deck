Audience: [persona] Goal: drive registrations or engagement Constraints: – Strong opening hook (first line must stop scrolling) – 1 clear insight (not generic) – Natural CTA – Max 100 words – No buzzwords Write 3 variations with different hooks.

Epic
Tap to flip
Campaign Challenger
L4 · Strategise
Prompt
Act as a critical marketing strategist. Challenge the campaign below. Campaign: [description] Audience: [persona] Goal: [objective] Evaluate: – Is the messaging differentiated or generic? – What assumptions are being made? – What would a sceptical buyer question? – Where is this weak or unclear? Provide: – 3 major risks or weaknesses – 2 ways to strengthen the positioning – 1 sharper, more compelling angle Be direct. Do not be polite.
Pro Tips
  • Use before launching any campaign.
  • Invite disagreement, not validation.
  • If it feels comfortable, it is probably weak.
